Step 2: Find the Paintings

Interact with 4 paintings around the map. The location of these painting are;

  • In the Trophy Room near the KRM-262 wallbuy, connected to the Upper Courtyard.
  • In the Clock Tower (Radio Room) directly before the spiral staircase inside.
  • Next to the barrier between Samantha's room and the Power Switch Room (Armory).
  • On the balcony inside the Control Room closest to Power Switch Room (Armory).

Interact with each painting in this specific order:

  • The Wolf King on his throne with his wolves.
  • The Wolf King with his helmet on and riding his horse.
  • A mountain with a castle and lightning.
  • The Wolf King slouched by his shield, with Arthur next to him.
