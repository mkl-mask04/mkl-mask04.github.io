Step 9: Completed Bow

After collecting the reforged arrow, you must fill it with souls to obtain the upgraded bow.

Take it to and place it in the Wolf marked box next to the Pyramid. 20 zombies must now be killed on the small stairs around the Pyramid or on the lower ground the Pyramid is situated upon.

When complete, place the Wrath of the Ancients into the box. After a few seconds, the upgraded Bow will be ready to pick up.
