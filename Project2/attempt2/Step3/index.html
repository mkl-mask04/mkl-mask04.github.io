Step 3: Collect the Arrow

After completing the painting step, go and collect the broken arrow.

Head into the Undercroft via the Family Crypt connected to Mission Control.

Once you reach the bottom of the staircase, the wolf symbol on the wall near the Pyramid area will automatically crumble, revealing the broken arrow which you can collect and continue to the following step.
