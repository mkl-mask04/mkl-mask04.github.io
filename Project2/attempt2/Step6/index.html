Step 1: Obtain The First Bow

Feed all three dragon head statues 8 zombies. The statues are located at

  • In the courtyard near Double Tap Root Beer, located above the tunnel that leads to the underground lab.
  • In Mission Control where Speed Cola is, located at the back of the room above a fireplace.
  • The last one is located in the Undercroft Pyramid room, across from the crafting bench.

Grab the Wrath of the Ancients bow from the room right above the undercroft where a knight is resting. It will be floating above the crate in the back of the room.
