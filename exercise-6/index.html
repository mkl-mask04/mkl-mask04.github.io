Synopsis

Welcome to the world of Charade, a thrilling romantic comedy that has captivated audiences for generations. Directed by Stanley Donen and starring Audrey Hepburn and Cary Grant, this film captures timeless charm against the enchanting streets of Paris.

Plot

The story begins in Paris, where Regina Lampert (Audrey Hepburn) returns from a skiing trip to find her husband murdered. Soon, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue as three mysterious men pursue her for a fortune her husband had stolen during World War II.

Regina's only ally is Peter Joshua (Cary Grant), a charming stranger who offers his help. But as the plot unfolds, his true intentions are unclear. With twists, betrayals, and suspense around every corner, Charade keeps audiences guessing until the very end.
